About Chancity Sports Club

Ready To Fight - Building Champions Since Day One

Home / About Us

Our Mission

Chancity Sports Club is dedicated to developing young athletes into disciplined, skilled, and competitive Kabaddi players. We believe in the transformative power of sports to build character, foster teamwork, and create lasting bonds between players, coaches, and the community.

"Ready To Fight" - This motto defines everything we do. It represents our unwavering determination, our courage to face any challenge, and our commitment to always give our absolute best, both on and off the mat.

As the official Kabaddi team of a Karnataka Government School, we operate under the guidelines and sanctioning of the Karnataka Sports Federation, ensuring that our athletes receive quality training and legitimate competitive opportunities.

Our Core Values

The principles that shape our athletes and define our culture

Teamwork

Kabaddi is the ultimate team sport. We emphasize collective effort over individual glory. Our players learn to trust each other, communicate effectively, and work together towards common goals. Success comes when we support one another.

Discipline

Champions are forged through discipline. Our training emphasizes consistency, punctuality, respect for coaches and teammates, and adherence to rules. Discipline on the mat translates to discipline in life.

Competitiveness

We play to win. Our motto "Ready To Fight" embodies our competitive spirit. We approach every match with determination and never back down from a challenge. Competition drives us to continuously improve.

Our History

The journey of Chancity Sports Club

Chancity Sports Club was founded with a vision to promote Kabaddi among young students and provide them with a platform to develop their athletic potential. What started as a small group of enthusiastic players has grown into a well-organized club with a strong reputation in Karnataka's sporting community.

Over the years, we have participated in numerous tournaments, won accolades, and most importantly, helped countless young athletes discover their passion for the sport. Our annual Chancity Open Kabaddi Tournament has become a prestigious event in the regional sporting calendar.

Today, Chancity Sports Club stands as a beacon for aspiring Kabaddi players, offering professional training, competitive opportunities, and a pathway to sports excellence. We continue to grow, learn, and evolve, always staying true to our core values and our motto: Ready To Fight.

Official Affiliation

Recognized and sanctioned for competitive play

Karnataka Government School

Chancity Sports Club is the official Kabaddi team representing our Karnataka Government School. We are proud to carry the school's banner in various inter-school and state-level competitions.

Karnataka Sports Federation

Our club operates under the sanctioning of the Karnataka Sports Federation, ensuring that all our activities, tournaments, and training programs meet official standards and guidelines.
