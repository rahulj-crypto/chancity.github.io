Tournament Format & Rules

Fair play, competitive spirit, and exciting matches

Format

The tournament follows a league-cum-knockout format. Teams are divided into groups for the initial rounds, followed by knockout stages leading to the grand finale. Each match consists of two halves of 20 minutes each.

Rules

All matches follow standard Kabaddi Federation rules. Teams must have 7 players on the mat at all times. Official referees oversee all matches to ensure fair play and adherence to regulations.

Eligibility

Open to all registered Kabaddi teams and school/college teams. Age categories apply for junior divisions. Valid ID and registration documents required. Team rosters must be submitted before the deadline.

Tournament Categories

Multiple divisions for competitive balance

Senior Category

  • Open to players aged 18 and above
  • Maximum 12 players per team roster
  • 7 players on mat, 5 substitutes
  • Standard mat dimensions (13m x 10m)
  • 40-minute matches (two 20-min halves)

The premier category featuring the best Kabaddi talent in the region. Intense competition and highest level of play.

Junior Category

  • For players under 18 years of age
  • Maximum 10 players per team roster
  • 7 players on mat, 3 substitutes
  • Modified mat dimensions as applicable
  • 30-minute matches (two 15-min halves)

Nurturing young talent and preparing future champions. Age verification documents required for all junior participants.
